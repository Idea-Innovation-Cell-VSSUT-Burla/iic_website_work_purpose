2. MAKER FEST

Read about the event

INTRODUCTION:

Maker Fest is a festival that celebrates the spirit of making, innovation, and creativity. It is a platform for makers, tinkerers, inventors, and artists to showcase their projects, ideas, and skills to the world. The festival brings together a diverse community of individuals who are passionate about making things and who believe in the power of hands-on learning and collaboration. Whether you're an experienced maker or just curious about the world of making, Maker Fest is the perfect place to get inspired, learn new skills, and connect with others who share your passions. So come on down and be a part of this exciting celebration of making, where you'll discover the limitless potential of your creativity and imagination!


EVENT FORMAT:

  • Mode: Offline
  • Location/Platform: Ground beside the Visvesvaraya Auditorium
  • Date : 4 th March 2023 – 6 th March 2023

    Exhibitor fair: The exhibitor fair is the main showcase of the festival, where participants can see the latest and greatest projects from makers, tinkerers, and artists. The exhibitor fair is a great opportunity for attendees to connect with makers and learn about their projects and techniques

"Bring your innovative ideas to life with "THINK TANK". Explore new frontiers, tackle challenges, and demonstrate your skills. Don't stop thinking about it, join us at THINK TANK and realize your potential”

"Unleash your creativity at "THINK TANK". Present your innovative ideas on chosen topics in 15 minutes and engage in group discussions with the jury. Be judged on your approach, presentation skills, and originality. Turn your ideas into reality at "THINK TANK".

  • 10:15am - Presentation Round
